⚠ Before each session

Cue-induced craving immediately before stimulation. (Frequency / %MT not specified in the source.)

Efficacy — pivotal RCT (n=262)

4-wk continuous quit 19.4% vs 8.7% sham (completers 28.0% vs 11.7%). First device ever FDA-cleared for smoking cessation.

Strength of evidence by indication — strongest → weakest; not all cleared/used indications are equal

1OCD (H7) — strongest sham-controlled signal; retains efficacy after med + CBT failure.
2Substance use (H4) — largest effect size across indications (SMD 1.16, GRADE moderate); smoking cleared, alcohol/stimulants investigational.
3Major depression (H1) — moderate and real, but not shown superior to cheaper focal (figure-8) rTMS.
4Anxious depression — 2021 label expansion on comorbid-anxiety data; no dedicated trial.
5PTSD — pivotal RCT negative (n=125; sham numerically better, p=0.027) — not established. Bipolar/schizophrenia/autism remain investigational.
CRITICAL SAFETY — SCREEN & AVOID

🚫 Implanted hardware near the coil

Ferromagnetic or active devices — aneurysm clips, cochlear implants, some pacemakers / neurostimulators — are a contraindication.

🚫 Seizure precautions

Screen for uncontrolled epilepsy, sleep deprivation, alcohol / benzodiazepine withdrawal, and seizure-threshold-lowering medications; stay within IFCN dosing limits. Seizures are rare but reported.

⚠ "Deeper" ≠ better

Deep TMS is not shown superior to standard focal (figure-8) rTMS, which is cheaper; a network meta-analysis found deep rTMS not > sham while HF-rTMS was.

⚠ Don't over-extend indications

PTSD pivotal was negative; the anxious-depression clearance rests on comorbid-anxiety data, not a dedicated trial. Match the coil to its cleared use.
