# Psychopharmacology Reference MOC

Cross-drug quick-reference material for prescribing psychopharmacology — dose ranges paired with
receptor pharmacology. Distinct from the decision reports and comprehensive reviews: these are
look-up tables and reference sheets, not adjudicated questions or whole-disorder chapters.

## Artifacts

- [[psychotropic-dose-receptor-occupancy|Antipsychotics & Antidepressants — Dose Range and Receptor Occupancy Reference]] — usual adult oral dose vs primary-target occupancy (striatal D2 for antipsychotics; SERT/NET/DAT for antidepressants), the 65–80% D2 window and ~80% SERT threshold, secondary receptors, and doses/combinations to avoid. 14 PET / systematic-review sources.
