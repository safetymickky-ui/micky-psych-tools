and ICD-11: nosology, epidemiology, stress-response etiology, clinical features, assessment
(IADQ/ADNM-20), differential diagnosis, treatment (psychotherapy, pharmacology, digital,
watchful waiting, and the suicide question), special populations, and prognosis. New reports
