# Neuromodulation MOC

Map of content for the neuromodulation modalities in psychiatry — the non-pharmacological,
device-based treatments: transcranial magnetic stimulation (rTMS and Deep TMS), and, as this
hub grows, ECT, tDCS, deep brain stimulation, and vagus-nerve stimulation. Reviews and
decision reports on brain-stimulation devices, their protocols, mechanisms, and evidence hang
here. New notes on stimulation targets, coil physics, or indication-specific protocols link
back to this map.

## Artifacts

- [[deep-transcranial-magnetic-stimulation-comprehensive-review]] — Whole-modality comprehensive review of Deep TMS (Brainsway H-coil), carried through a ten-section arc adapted from disorder to treatment-technology: device class and the H1/H7/H4 coil family, the four FDA clearances (major depression 2013, OCD 2018, smoking cessation 2020, anxious depression 2021), H-coil field physics (supra-threshold to ~4–5 cm vs ~1.5 cm for figure-8, depth bought at the cost of focality) and the network-spread mechanism, the acute course and patient selection, comparators (the unresolved dTMS-vs-standard-rTMS question — a network meta-analysis found deep rTMS *not* superior to sham while HF-rTMS was), protocols and efficacy by indication (MDD pivotal n=212 remission 32.6 vs 14.6%; OCD pivotal n=99 response 38.1 vs 11.1%; smoking pivotal n=262 quit 19.4 vs 8.7% — with OCD and substance-use the strongest signals and PTSD a *negative* pivotal), special populations, durability/maintenance, and the addiction-heavy pipeline plus a single-sponsor reporting-transparency flag. 42 PubMed sources + 17 registry trials. Confidence moderate; not a patient-level verdict.
