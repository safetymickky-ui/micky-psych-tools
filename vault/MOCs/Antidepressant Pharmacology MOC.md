# Antidepressant Pharmacology MOC

Map of content for the mechanistic pharmacology of antidepressants and their selection —
serotonin receptor map, transporter/occupancy, pharmacokinetics vs pharmacodynamics,
adverse-effect phenotypes (activation, akathisia, emotional blunting), the α₂δ ligands as the
non-monoamine option, and misuse/dependence/withdrawal. Reports and notes that reason from
*mechanism to drug choice* hang here. Disorder-specific treatment algorithms live under
[[Depression — Treatment MOC]] and the anxiety MOCs; this MOC is the drug-side, cross-disorder
pharmacology reference.

## Artifacts

- [[antidepressant-selection-by-mechanism]] — Selection instrument: efficacy is a class near-tie (occupancy plateaus at ~80% SERT), so choose by mechanism matched to phenotype — receptor fingerprint → side-effect forecast, half-life → discontinuation risk, α₂δ ligands as the non-monoamine anxiety option (capped by dependence).
