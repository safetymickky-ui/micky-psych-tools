# Primary Headache Disorders MOC

Map of content for the primary headache disorders — tension-type headache and migraine
above all, with medication-overuse headache as their shared secondary complication and
cluster headache / the trigeminal autonomic cephalalgias in the differential. Covers the
full arc: ICHD-3 nosology, epidemiology and the prevalence/disability inversion,
neurovascular vs myofascial pathophysiology, clinical features, clinical diagnosis and red
flags, the TTH↔migraine differential and comorbidity (stroke, mood), treatment across
acute/preventive/neuromodulation/behavioral modalities (triptans, gepants, ditans, CGRP
antibodies, onabotulinumtoxinA, amitriptyline), special populations, prognosis and
chronification, and the migraine-vs-TTH research-attention asymmetry. New reports and notes
on headache hang here.
